Number of jobs in Feb 2020

It is important to also consider the number of jobs (job count) and classify industries into small, medium or large sized industries (ideally the ABS would already have a classification system).

#-----filter 2020-05-01 counts-----  
covid_feb_labour_count <- tidy_labour %>%
  filter(date == as.Date("2020-05-01")) %>%
  filter(sub_industry == "None",
         gender == "total",

  select(date,
         count,
         main_industry) %>%
  mutate(count_summary =  case_when(between(count, 100, 250) ~ "100-250 thousand",
                                    between(count, 250.001, 500) ~ "250-500 thousand",
